Day One Revenue

Real Property Token has been created and designed to use blockchain technology to assist everybody with their real estate transactions, our services fit into 3 groups

Transaction Fees - Include any payment that is normally made using fiat currency. It can be for listing fees, advertising fees, rent, legal fees, maintenance, management, accounting and other business services. The goal of Real Property Token is to implement our innovative solutions on a global scale reducing real estate transaction fees whilst introducing a wider audience to all people listing a property through our free listing App

Education - Taking a company globally means you need a competitive edge, you need brand awareness, you quite simply need to be the best, and when you are the best, then you need to showcase that. Real Property Token will build brand awareness with roadshows around the world teaching anybody that wants to know, how cryptocurrencies work. This is an exciting space, and we want to share it, we know we are the best, and we are going to showcase that

Investments - Low cost, high yielding investments will available to Real Property Token holders. The first investment opportunities have already been created and will be available to investors using their tokens as soon as the ICO is complete and our legal team have completed the necessary documents. Utilizing our Crowd Decision App all Real Property Token holders will together be able to make decisions on all investment opportunities

Initial Coin Offering (ICO)

To implement Real Property Tokens vision, we are launching an initial coin offering (ICO) to issue Real Property tokens on the public blockchain. The tokens called RPTokens (RPT) will be issued during the pre-ICO and ICO. The pre-ICO will be launched to allow an early-bird discount for those people that have been following our progress over the years. We have capped the tokens availabe during the pre-sale to 50%. The RPTokens (RPT) after the ICO, will be traded on cryptocurrency exchanges to provide liquidity for token holders.

One of the biggest errors that our research has uncovered is that many token projects do not have a clear roadmap to produce a revenue stream immediately after their ICO and we noticed that many of these token offerings had to go a further round of funding to reach their first milestone on the roadmap. This creates FUD which in turn lowers the value of the token and entices market makers to attempt to control the market for the token to profit. With our clear and precise vision towards creating a revenue stream from the first milestone we believe we have overcome this problem and this in turn will create greater stability for our token.

Each holder of RPTokens (RPT) will be entitled to an invite to each project that Real Property Token undertakes. This means that token holders can be part of a crypto mining operation in Europe, a charity in Africa, a holiday apartment in Boracay(one of the most beautiful holiday locations on the planet), a property development in New Zealand, mobile APPS that are used by the business and available for public download.

Finally, our goal is to run our organization in a decentralized manner allowing token holders to have input in projects whilst at the same time allowing them to profit from them. Using the available technology our end game is to make Real Property Token the cryptocurrency of choice for all property settlements. We know we will get there if we build the right brand awareness and always keep it real.
